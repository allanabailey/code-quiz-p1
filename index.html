How to Play

  1. Choose which coding language you would like to be quizzed on by clicking on the four buttons at the top.
  2. Read the question carefully.
  3. Type your answer into the text box. Don't worry about upper or lower case - it doesn't matter!
  4. Hit Enter on your keyboard or click the submit button.
  5. Try to get as many questions correctly as you can! There are 10 questions for each programming language.
  6. Refresh the page to try again, or click on a new quiz type once you have answered all 10 questions for the chosen quiz type.
  7. You can change between the quiz types and return back at any stage.
