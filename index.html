How to Play

  1. Choose which coding language you would like to be quizzed on by clicking on the four buttons at the top.
  2. Read the question carefully.
  3. Type your answer into the text box. Don't worry about upper or lower case - it doesn't matter!
  4. Hit Enter on your keyboard or click the submit button.
  5. Try to get all 10 questions correct.
  6. Refresh the page to try again, or click on a new quiz type!
